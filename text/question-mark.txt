first i called it a crush, then came the word love, and the newly invented situationship thing, but the truth is i don't know what the heck is going on between us. 
he's the best and the worst. 
he's nothing and yet everything. 
when i forget about him, i remember everything else around us. 

what does it mean to flirt with someone, then take it all back the next day ? 
why does he do it ? 
when will it stop ? 
where do i draw the line ? 

well doesn't matter where i draw the line,
i did it with a pencil, and i keep erasing it, to push it further.
i may be masochistic at this point. 
or maybe i'm just a prisoner of the cage i built all on my own.

forced to listen to the same songs again, 
to play the same games, 
to live the same thing over and over again, 
until one of us breaks. 

he's an ass, doesn't ask anything about me. 
he's the cutest, wishing me happy birthday. 
he's disappeared again, i worry again. 
he comes back, i'm mad. 

truth is, i don't know him that much. 
i don't see what he does all day, all night. 
we were once close, yet so far, 
now we're just apart. 

i miss him, 
i love him, 
and i can't say anything about it. 

why, oh why did you came into my life that night ?
what went through my mind when i texted you back ? 
when did we grew apart ? 
where did i mess it all up ?

we were. 
maybe, one day, we'll be.
