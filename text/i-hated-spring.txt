I hate spring.
it's my least favorite season because nothing feels right at this time.
it has always been this way and I don't really know why.
I had my most terrible experiences during this season, so I never associate it with anything likeable.
until then.
until everything went faster, weirder, but better.
it felt like I was finding another part of myself. like the puzzle was piecing itself together.
whether it's that moment where silence said a lot, or the way you looked at me before you told me something you never told anyone about. it felt right.

it wasn't some feeling that would fade away in a month, or a year. no, it was something that I would feel everyday in my stomach, in my heart.
that formula one race going on and on every time you dared to cross my mind.
you and your horribly magical smile, your annoyingly gorgeous hair, and this weirdly likeable attitude you got.

whenever I go back to that spring, I just fall deeper than last time.
to think we were here, together, at a such a beautiful place, and that I couldn't breathe properly thinking about it the night it happened.
the moment it clicked. I was falling in love again, the moment I had decided to wait for college to think about love.
you were there, being absolute perfection.

now, when I go see the pictures, I see everything again. that movie poster we walked by, this melted ice cream pot I gave you, the monuments we took pictures of. I remember it all.
it was an awaken dream.
maybe I hated spring.
maybe now I can say that I like it.

but mostly, I like you.
